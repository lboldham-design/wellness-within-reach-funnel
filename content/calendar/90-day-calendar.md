## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC NOTES

### Instagram
- Post feed + Reel for video content (same day)
- Stories: share each post with a poll ("save this?" / "does this resonate?")
- Highlights: organize by theme (Breathwork, ADHD, Parents, Burnout, Daily Reset)
- Bio: rotating CTA between free guide and toolkit based on week

### TikTok
- Video content only (V series)
- Captions: first line is the hook (appears on screen)
- Trending audio: use calm lo-fi or nature sounds
- Post between 8–10am or 6–9pm local time
- Reply to comments with video responses for Week 12 launch

### Pinterest
- Quote posts + carousels (Q and C series)
- Save to boards: "Nervous System Tips," "ADHD Wellness," "Burnout Recovery," "Breathing Techniques," "Parenting Wellness"
- Pin descriptions: include full caption + keyword-rich description
- Optimal frequency: 3–5 pins/day including repins

### Facebook / Facebook Group
- Quote posts and carousel posts
- Week 6 (parenting content): highest engagement on Facebook
- Boost posts in weeks 12–13 during launch

### LinkedIn (Professional content)
- Weeks 7, 9: highest relevance (professional burnout, HRV, polyvagal)
- Adapt captions to remove emojis or reduce them
- CTA: "Free guide for professionals — link in bio/comments"
