## Copywriting Notes

- **Voice:** Warm, honest, science-informed. Not clinical, not "woo." Talk to a tired, smart person who has heard too many wellness promises.
- **Story before pitch:** Emails 1–3 are 100% value. Email 4 introduces the product softly. Email 5 is the close.
- **Avoid:** Scarcity tactics that feel fake, excessive exclamation points, toxic positivity ("Just choose joy!"), or anything that implies the reader is broken.
- **Amplify:** Permission ("you don't have to do it all"), self-compassion, biological normalizing, specificity over vagueness.
- **The trust sequence:** By Day 5, readers feel seen, understood, and like they've already received value. The offer feels like a natural next step, not a sales pitch.
